Döner

Description

Döner is a traditional Turkish dish made with a spiced ground meat that is cooked on a vertical rotisserie. The meat is marinated in a mixture of spices and then cooked until it is tender and juicy. The meat is then sliced off the rotisserie and served in a pita bread with various toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of ground meat
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 1 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground meat, water, oil, flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Place the meat on a baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the meat from the oven and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  6. Slice the meat and serve with various toppings.
